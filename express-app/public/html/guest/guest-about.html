ABOUT US

Our Story

Our journey began with the vision to bridge the gap between clinical narratives and accessible medical insights. We recognized that vital information often remains hidden in prescriptions and notes, making it difficult for healthcare professionals to interpret efficiently.

By combining OCR, NLP, and BioBERT NER, we developed a system that transforms unstructured text into clear, actionable knowledge. This empowers healthcare providers to access accurate data with ease and enhances decision-making for better patient outcomes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to harness the power of technology to decode complex clinical narratives and make vital healthcare information more accessible. We aim to reduce barriers caused by unstructured medical text and ensure that insights are clear, accurate, and actionable for healthcare providers.

By integrating OCR, NLP, and BioBERT NER, we strive to deliver a system that enhances medical decision-making, supports patient safety, and drives innovation in the healthcare industry. Through this, we hope to contribute to a future where technology empowers better care for everyone.

OUR SOLUTION

Our solution combines OCR technology with advanced NLP models to seamlessly convert prescriptions and clinical notes into digital text. This process ensures that important medical details such as medicines and dosages are captured with accuracy and reliability.

Through the use of BioBERT NER, the system identifies medicine names and retrieves their related information including drug relations, prices, and side effects. This provides structured medical insights that help healthcare professionals make faster and safer decisions.
